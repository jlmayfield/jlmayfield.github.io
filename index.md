I am the Commander G.E. Boone Endowed Professor of Computational Sciences in the [School of Engineering, Sciences, and Innovation](https://www.monmouthcollege.edu/school-of-engineering-sciences-and-innovation/) at [Monmouth College](http://www.monmouthcollege.edu).
